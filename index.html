About Us

Atlntix Exports is an India-Based company that deals in the cattle feeds, cattle medicines, fresh vegetables, fruits, grains, milk products, pulses, spices, cooking oil, eggs and many more products like frozen foods and vegetables. Company established in the year 2025, having headquartered at Pune, Maharastra. Currently functioning in state of Maharashtra with plans to become international. Meets all worldwide quality standards along with FSSAI certifications. Quality and sustainability are at the heart of our operations.

Empowering the Future with Clear Vision and Bold Goals

Vision

To be the world's most successful and important export company. Successful in helping our customers to get the best products easily. To be a globally recognized leader in the superfoods industry, dedicated to enhancing health and well-being through natural, nutrient-rich products.

Goals

Our mission is to inspire healthier living by providing the finest quality superfoods that nourish the body and promote sustainable well-being. We are committed to sourcing and delivering natural products.

Mission

Our goal is to establish a noticable presence in both domestic and international markets
through the provision and services.
